SAA PenPal Challenges

February 5th, 2021

⛈⛈⛈⛈⛈Breaking News⛈⛈⛈⛈⛈

The time is finally here!

Good afternoon, my name is Jessica Olivas and currently serve as this term Sergeant At Arms Chair for District Convention 2021 and do I have a forecast for you. This upcoming District Convention will be full of surprises and fun, and one way for you to be involved is to participate in the PenPal Challenges. The PenPal Challenges will be you completing in a team with two other partners to answer questions and complete interactive activities. This pairing is a way to meet individuals from across the district, and form new friendships. And while it may seem a bit lonely on your computer, fear not as there will be some new friends for you to hang out with, so even if you are in Hawaii you might get to hangout with a member in Las Vegas. For any further questions please come by Office Hours on February 8th, at 7PM PST / 5PM HST. So buckle up and prepare to be blown away by all the challenges you and your partners will face! Deadline: Saturday, February 27th at 11:59PM PST / 9:59 PM HST.

District Convention is recruiting you to be our workshop host. Weather you have experience with leadership roles or can share some of your cool skills with the members of CNH, apply now!
