What is District Convention?

The California-Nevada-Hawaii District Circle K District hosts an annual three day and two night event called District Convention for clubs all around the district at Los Angeles, California. Attendees get to learn more about all levels of Circle K International, network with professionals, and educate themselves on how to become aspiring leaders. District Convention is an event where attendees get together and celebrate all of the achievements of the district and acknowledging all club’s hard work. Join Circle K at our 68th annual CNH District Convention online from March 25th to 27th, 2022.
