What is District Convention?

District Convention 2021

The California-Nevada-Hawaii District Circle K District hosts an annual three day and two night event called District Convention for clubs all around the district at Riverside, California. Attendees get to learn more about all levels of Circle K International, networking with professionals, and be educated to become aspiring leaders. District Convention is an event where attendees get together and celebrate all of the achievements of the district and acknowledging all club’s hard work. Join Circle K at our 67th annual CNH District Convention at the Riverside Convention Center from March 19th to 22nd, 2021.
